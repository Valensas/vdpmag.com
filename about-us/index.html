About Valensas

Founded in 2006, Valensas is a mobile technology company with a focus in Apple platforms. We develop mobile applications and integrate services that ensure our clients stay on cutting edge technology. Our products and services allow industry leaders to acquire new customers through mobile channels and enable for business productivity and growth.

Valensas is the developer of many popular iOS (Apple) applications and business solutions for leading companies in the telecom, finance, pharmaceutical, FMCG and automotive industries.

Valensas has offices in Istanbul and New York City and an R&D Center in Teknokent, Istanbul.

Specialties

Mobile, iOS, iPhone, iPad, Apple, Application Development, Mobile Enterprise, eDetailing, Digital Publishing, Magazine Publishing, Mobile Communication

About VDP

Started in July 2010, shortly after the introduction of the first iPad, Valensas launched the first iPad issue in Turkey with LOG magazine.  Since then VDP has been growing both locally and worldwide with over 45 titles and more than 400 magazines available for download 24/7, worldwide. Ease of use and single pricing model has given VDP today’s position. VDP will continue to improve its technology and infrastructure necessary to provide publishers with next-generation publishing needs and the readers a better media experience.
