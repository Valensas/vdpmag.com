Mobile Publishing Made Easier with VDP 2.0

VDP 2.0 makes it even easier to publish magazines to iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch devices with second generation publishing platform.

Social media pitch

Mobile Publishing is now even easier with new generation VDP via @vdpmag for details visit vdpmag.com

Summary

Valensas Technology, a well known mobile tech company based in Istanbul, released its new generation mobile publishing platform VDP 2.0. The new platform makes it even easier to publish magazines to iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch devices without developing any code. The new platform comes with a more user friendly environment, usability and features.

Details

Developed by Valensas Technology, one of Turkeyâ€™s first mobile startups, Valensas Digital Publishing (VDP) version 2.0 was announced earlier in March at a publisher exclusive workshop demonstrating new features and updates. The platform is now publicly available to all users and free to download on the App Store and vdpmag.com.

VDP allows content owners to publish to iPhone, iPad and iPod touch devices without developing any code. With drag & drop simplicity, instead of dealing with technical issues publishers can focus on crafting the next big mobile magazine including, interactive content, and mobile revenue streams. The new platform enables for even easier publishing through a better user interface, interactive design widgets, file extension and push notifications. The user friendly environment allow publishers to start publishing without the need of technical support or training.

A major difference would be the pricing structure and support for Newsstand. In average VDP costs much cheaper than most rivals and allows publishers to take place on Newsstand with their own brand and Apple certificate allowing for faster results and better appearance of the brand.

Currently there are over 500 issues available for download on VDP, including the first iPad magazine in Turkey published in 2010, shortly after the launch of the first generation iPad. Over 300.000 mobile readers generate over 20 million unique sessions through VDP. The total amount of magazine downloads have exceeded 750 thousand requests. VDP is expected to become cross-platform in the middle of 2013 with Android support and Windows following short after.
