Here’s what you need to begin

  • VDP Manager

    Mac OS X Mountain Lion (v10.8) with VDP Manager App installed

    Download on Desktop OS X 10.7+

  • VDP Viewer

    iPad with VDP Viewer App installed (iPad 2/iPhone 3GS or above)

    Download on the App Store

  • Apple Certificate

    Apple Certificate to distribute your application to the App Store.

    developer.apple.com

It is recommended that every company applies for own certificate. However this can wait until your publication is ready for final distribution.
You can obtain a certificate simply by following the step-by-step guide at developer.apple.com .
