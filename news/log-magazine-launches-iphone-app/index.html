  • LOG Magazine launches iPhone app via VDP

    March 3rd, 2013

    SHARE Twitter Facebook

    Turkey’s first iPad magazine LOG is now available for iPhone and iPod Touch. LOG is a leading consumer electronics magazine which made an early transition into tablet media. Since its first launch on iPad in 2010 LOG is growing further into digital media and everything mobile. With over 50 available issues on Apple’s Newsstand LOG is aiming high not only in terms of new media but also in mobile advertising and content development. We’re all excited to see LOG’s next move, congratulations to the team and everyone who participated in such great content.
