    Mobile Publishing Made Easier with VDP 2.0

  • VDP grows network with Doğan Burda Group

    March 29th, 2013

    SHARE Twitter Facebook

    Turkey’s biggest magazine publisher Doğan Burda chose VDP to migrate all of its magazines to the iPad. The app, DergiBurada, contains 23 magazine titles within a single library. The app contains local editions of globally known titles such as Elle, Maison Française, Popular Science, Capital, AutoShow, Chip, Level magazines as well as popular locals like Atlas, Istanbul Life, Tempo, Lezzet, EvBahçe and many more. DergiBurada offers a wide selection of titles ranging from food to automotive and fashion to business & economics reaching readers from almost every age and interest group.

    DergiBurada is also the first generation of VDP which allows a single publisher to organize and sell all its publications around a central repository. Publishers with more than one title can benefit from this great VDP feature and build their own custom branded Newsstand App
