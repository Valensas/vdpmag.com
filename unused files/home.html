More Features



  • .vdp File Extension

    VDP Manager has a document based work environment allowing to work on multiple workstations using the .vdp file extension.

  • Portrait vs. Landscape

    Customize content to portrait and landscape layouts and let your readers choose for the best viewing experience.

  • Printable Pages

    Print pages with a touch of a button. Pages are organized in a printer-friendly order.

  • Bookmark

    Add bookmarks to favorite pages and easily find content with a single touch. You can also bookmark a page and continue reading on other devices.

  • Restore

    Restore all previous purchases when you switch to another iOS device with click of a button.

  • Integration

    We integrate to your preferred analytics tool (Omniture, Flurry, Google Analytics, etc) for your convenience.

  • Duplicate Action

    You can easily duplicate a page with all actions included saving you a lot of time and trouble when creating a similar page.

  • Catalog View

    Ideal for publishers with several magazines, catalog view allows you to store all your magazines in an organized fashion.

  • Custom Video Player

    You can view videos within a page or in full screen for maximum results. Customize your video player according to your preferences.

  • in-App Online Purchasing

    Purchase online without leaving the app. You can guide readers to landing pages with direct purchase links of products and services.

  • Apple Certificate

    Publish using your own Apple Certificate. We encourage publishers to use their company info in the App Store instead of developer’s.
